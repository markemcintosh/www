Who am I?

I am an Operating Partner/Investor

My main work is Raising debt and sometimes equity for Portfolio companies

Private Equity for short. I used to work at McKinsey and Company. I went to Harvard College and Harvard Law School.

I like Art

I like art shows and museums and the ocasional piece of fine art.

I like music

I was the head of the Black Music Department at Harvard's radio station for a few years and I used to DJ parties. Now I just listen.

I like to learn new things

Most recently, I have been learning about blockchain technology and have spent time implementing smart contracts on Solana and Ethereum testnets. Buildspace has great tutorials.

I like to invest in startups

I have invested in a few startups and have been a mentor for a few startups. Some investments are direct, others via syndicates.

I like the business of fashion

I used to be known as a nice dresser. Now I just wear clothes. I do things like COmplexcon and Hypefest but I am not a sneakerhead. I used to work in the fashion buying and planning department at Macy's
